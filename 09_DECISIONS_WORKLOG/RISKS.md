# Risk Register

## Purpose

This document tracks risks that may impact QA automation work, including mitigation strategies and ownership.

## Risk Format

Each risk should include:
- **Risk ID**: Unique identifier
- **Description**: Clear description of the risk
- **Category**: Type of risk
- **Probability**: High/Medium/Low
- **Impact**: High/Medium/Low
- **Risk Level**: Calculated risk level
- **Mitigation**: Mitigation strategies
- **Owner**: Risk owner
- **Status**: Active/Mitigated/Closed
- **Date Identified**: When risk was identified
- **Last Updated**: Last update date

## Risk Assessment

### Risk Level Calculation

| Probability | Impact | Risk Level |
|-------------|--------|------------|
| High | High | High |
| High | Medium | High |
| High | Low | Medium |
| Medium | High | High |
| Medium | Medium | Medium |
| Medium | Low | Low |
| Low | High | Medium |
| Low | Medium | Low |
| Low | Low | Low |

### Risk Response Strategies

#### High Risk
- Immediate action required
- Escalate to management
- Develop detailed mitigation plan
- Regular monitoring

#### Medium Risk
- Develop mitigation plan
- Monitor regularly
- Review periodically

#### Low Risk
- Accept or monitor
- Review as needed

## Risk Review

### Review Frequency
- **High Risk**: Weekly
- **Medium Risk**: Monthly
- **Low Risk**: Quarterly

### Review Process
1. Assess current status
2. Update probability/impact if changed
3. Review mitigation effectiveness
4. Update mitigation strategies if needed
5. Close risks that are mitigated
