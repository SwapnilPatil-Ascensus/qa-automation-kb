# Meeting Notes 2026

## Purpose

This folder contains meeting notes for 2026. Meeting notes are organized by date and topic.

## Naming Convention

Meeting notes should be named: `YYYY-MM-DD_TOPIC.md`

Examples:
- `2026-01-25_Sprint_Planning.md`
- `2026-01-26_Daily_Standup.md`
- `2026-01-27_Release_Review.md`

## Meeting Notes Template

Use the following structure for meeting notes:

```markdown
# Meeting: [Topic] - [Date]

## Meeting Information
- **Date**: [Date]
- **Time**: [Time]
- **Duration**: [Duration]
- **Attendees**: [List of attendees]
- **Facilitator**: [Name]

## Agenda
1. [Agenda item 1]
2. [Agenda item 2]
3. [Agenda item 3]

## Discussion Points
### [Topic 1]
[Discussion notes]

### [Topic 2]
[Discussion notes]

## Decisions Made
- [Decision 1]
- [Decision 2]

## Action Items
| Action Item | Owner | Due Date | Status |
|-------------|-------|----------|--------|
| [Action 1] | [Owner] | [Date] | [Status] |
| [Action 2] | [Owner] | [Date] | [Status] |

## Next Steps
- [Next step 1]
- [Next step 2]

## Notes
[Any additional notes]
```

## Meeting Types

### Regular Meetings
- Daily Standup
- Weekly Team Meeting
- Sprint Planning
- Sprint Review
- Retrospective

### Ad-hoc Meetings
- Issue Resolution
- Design Discussions
- Process Improvements
- Knowledge Sharing

## Important Notes

- Document all decisions in `09_DECISIONS_WORKLOG/DECISIONS.md`
- Track action items in `11_BACKLOG/ACTION_ITEMS.md`
- Update relevant documentation based on meeting outcomes
